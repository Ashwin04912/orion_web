About Us

The Space Club of Govt Engineering College, Wayanad, is a community of aspiring space enthusiasts, engineers, and innovators. We explore the mysteries of the universe, conduct workshops, research projects, and inspire students to dream beyond the stars. Our mission is to foster curiosity and innovation in space technology and astronomy.
