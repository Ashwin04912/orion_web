About Us

ORION – The Space Club of Govt. Engineering College, Wayanad, is a community of students passionate about exploring the universe. We engage in space research, innovative projects, and knowledge-sharing activities, inspiring curiosity and innovation among budding engineers.

Our mission is to connect young minds with the wonders of space science and technology through workshops, research, and hands-on projects. Together, we aim to reach for the stars ✨.
