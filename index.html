Our Focus

We are dedicated to empowering students with innovation, technology, and creativity. Here's what we focus on and what we provide:

AI and ML

We champion AI and ML projects in space, guiding students to create smart, innovative solutions for analyzing satellite data and enabling autonomous operations.

RF Communication & Ham Radios

Students learn about radio frequency communication, practical applications with ham radios, and the fundamentals of wireless technology for space and ground-based projects.

Antenna Designing

We guide students through the process of designing, building, and testing antennas, a critical skill for effective space and terrestrial communication.

Space Robotics

Our workshops focus on space robotics, covering everything from robotic arm mechanics to autonomous navigation, essential for future space missions.

Community & Global Opportunities

We connect students with global tech communities, competitions, and collaborative projects, helping them apply their skills and expand their network.
